Controls

D-Pad: Move player

How to Play

  • Survive waves of enemies with your partner
  • Shoot enemies to earn points
  • Avoid enemy bullets and collisions
  • Both players must survive to continue
  • Game ends when both players lose all health

Scoring

  • Enemy Kill: 100 points
  • Wave Complete: 500 bonus
  • Health bonus per wave
